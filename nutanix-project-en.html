Project Overview

Executed comprehensive market research and lead generation project targeting 339 Korean government agencies to establish Nutanix Korea's public sector sales foundation. As an SDR, led the entire process from market entry strategy development to execution, leveraging ALIO public disclosure database for strategic market penetration in Korean government virtualization market.

Specific SDR Activities:

  • ✅
    Market Research & Targeting: Segmented and prioritized institutions across quasi-government agencies, public corporations, and other public organizations
  • ✅
    Decision Maker Mapping: Directly sourced and secured contact information for key stakeholders including IT managers, CIOs, and IT department heads at each institution
  • ✅
    Systematic Outbound Execution: Conducted structured needs assessment and pain point identification using MEDDPICC methodology-based surveys
  • ✅
    Pipeline Development: Generated 180+ qualified leads by comprehensively analyzing budget allocation, procurement timelines, and technical requirements

Key Market Insights

Market Segmentation Analysis

Existing Users (~60%)

  • Majority using VMware server/desktop virtualization
  • Pain Points: High licensing costs, system aging, performance degradation
Example: Korea Workers' Compensation & Welfare Service (KRW 5B budget, VMware 3-4 years, considering upgrade)

New Adoption Prospects (~40%)

  • Driven by server management efficiency and cost reduction
Example: Korea Social Enterprise Promotion Agency (KRW 1.5B dedicated budget, 2021 project completion target)

Procurement Process Characteristics

  • Procurement Method: G2B (Korean government e-procurement) public bidding dominates 90%+
  • Budget Cycle: Year-end budget planning, execution in following year H1/H2
  • Decision Structure:
    • Technical Evaluation: IT departments
    • Final Decision: External committees or institutional heads
